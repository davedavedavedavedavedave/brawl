About

The Munich AGoT meta invites you to its 2018 Game of Thrones LCG Joust tournament. Meet fellow Thrones enthusiasts - and crush them under your heel, laughing boisterously at their feeble attempts to check the indomitable juggernaut that is your deck. It is time to take up arms and assert your dominance in the Only Game That Matters!

Don't miss this awesome event and remember - Munich is lovely and always worth visiting. Why not make a nice weekend trip there, send your loved ones sightseeing and spend a few hours doing battle with your peers? It's gonna be legendary!

We'll once more have a bunch of awesome prizes, both original FFG merchandise and other great swag. No participant will leave the Inn at the Crossroads emptyhanded!

The admission fee will be € 10,- per head for the Joust tournament on Saturday.

Location

Jugendzentrum Gauting
Bahnhofstraße 6
82131 Gauting
Germany
