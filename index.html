About

The Munich AGoT meta invites you to its 2019 Game of Thrones LCG Joust tournament. The glorious Brawl at the Crossroads Inn is taking a break this year. Instead, we present you The Brawl at Corpse Lake! Make use of this unique opportunity to test for Stahleck in a tournament setting.

The admission fee will be € 10,- per head for the Joust tournament on Saturday.

Schedule

Registration for the Joust Tournament on Saturday, 26th of October begins at 10.30am. The tournament itself begins at 11.30am.

The Joust tournament will conclude on Sunday, 27th of October, beginning at 10am. Side events will also start on Sunday, 27th of October, begin at 10am.
